‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I optimize my delivery routes?
Simply add your delivery addresses to NaviBatch, and our intelligent algorithm will automatically calculate the most efficient route to save you time and fuel.
Can I import addresses from a file?
Yes! NaviBatch supports importing addresses from CSV files, Excel spreadsheets, and text files. You can also paste multiple addresses at once.
How does the batch navigation feature work?
Our patent-pending batch navigation technology allows you to navigate to multiple addresses with just one click, automatically switching between destinations as you complete each delivery.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your routes and delivery information are stored securely and never shared with third parties.
Can I use NaviBatch offline?
NaviBatch requires an internet connection for route optimization and real-time navigation. However, once your route is optimized, you can use Apple Maps for offline navigation.
